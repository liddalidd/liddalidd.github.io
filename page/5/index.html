Summaries for centre

  • mean (\miu x)

  • median (\hat x)
    left skewed: meanmedian

  • variance (Var(x))

  • standard deviation (sd(x))
  • quartiles (IQR)
    lower threshold: LT=Q1-1.5IQR
    upper threshold: UT=Q3+1.5IQR
    outlier : outsid [LT,UT]
    interquartile range: IQR=Q3-Q1
  • mean absolute deviation(MAD)
    $MAD=\frac{1}{n}\sum_{i=1}^n\lvert x_i-\overline{x}\rvert$
  • mean square error (MSE)
    $MSE=\frac{1}{n}\sum_{i=1}^n(x_i-\overline{x})^2$
