Density estimation

Likelihoood approach

likelihood: Sample -> Population
probability: Population -> Sample

Maximum likelihood approach:
maximise $L(\Theta |x)$

Non-parametric approach

Kernel functions

non-negative K(x) ≥ 0, symmetric K( − x) = K(x), unit measure ∫ K(x) dx = 1

Kernel density esimation

$\hat{ f(x) }=\frac{1}{nh} \sum_{i=1}^n K( \frac{X_i-x}{h})$

Choosing the bandwidth

A bias and variance trade-off.
A small bandwidth gives high variance
A large bandwidth gives high bias
